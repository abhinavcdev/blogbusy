# The Future of Vibecoding

We are just at the beginning of the Vibecoding revolution. As models get smarter and contexts get larger, the barrier between thought and software dissolves.

## The Death of Syntax Errors
In the future, syntax errors will be a relic of the dark ages. The AI will autocorrect logic and syntax in real-time. You will never miss a semicolon again because the concept of manually typing a semicolon will be obsolete.

## Pure Creation
Software engineering will become a pure creative art. We will describe worlds, systems, and interactions, and the machine will weave them into reality.
